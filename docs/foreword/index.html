Foreword¶

overreact allows you to easily construct and analyze microkinetic models. All data is parsed directly from logfiles thanks to cclib. Free energies calculated under the quasi-rigid rotor harmonic oscillator are employed in the automatic prediction of reaction rate constants (including tunneling effects). Reaction networks can be subsequently simulated over time.
